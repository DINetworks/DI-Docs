## Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have:

- [ ] **Compatible Wallet**: MetaMask, WalletConnect, or similar
- [ ] **Supported Network**: Connected to Ethereum, BSC, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base, or Crossfi
- [ ] **Base Tokens**: ETH, BNB, MATIC, or other native tokens for gas fees
- [ ] **Basic DeFi Knowledge**: Understanding of wallets, tokens, and transactions

## 5-Minute Setup

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit [app.dinetwork.xyz](https://app.dinetwork.xyz)
2. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right
3. Select your preferred wallet (MetaMask recommended)
4. Approve the connection request

### Step 2: Get DI Tokens

You need DI tokens to use the protocol:

**Option A: Buy on DEX**
- Use Uniswap, PancakeSwap, or other DEXs
- Search for DI token contract address
- Swap ETH/BNB/MATIC for DI tokens

**Option B: Participate in Token Sale**
- Check [dinetwork.xyz](https://dinetwork.xyz) for active sales
- Follow KYC requirements if applicable
- Purchase directly from the protocol

### Step 3: Mint DUSD Stablecoin

DUSD is the base currency for all trading:

1. Go to the "Mint" section in the app
2. Deposit DI tokens as collateral
3. Mint DUSD (up to 75% of collateral value)
4. Pay 5% APR interest on borrowed DUSD

### Step 4: Start Trading

Now you can access all protocol features:

**Synthetic Assets (DSwap)**
- Trade stocks: xAAPL, xTSLA, xGOOG
- Trade commodities: xGold, xSilver, xOil
- Trade crypto: xBTC, xETH, xBNB

**Perpetual Trading (DPerp)**
- Open leveraged positions up to 50x
- Go long or short on any asset
- Earn from both rising and falling markets

### Step 5: Earn Rewards

Multiple ways to earn passive income:

**Staking**
- Stake DI tokens for 8-20% APY
- Lock for longer periods for higher rewards
- Participate in governance voting

**Liquidity Provision**
- Provide DUSD to perpetual trading pools
- Earn trading fees and funding payments
- Receive DLP tokens representing your share

## Important Reminders

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Security First**
- Never share your private keys or seed phrase
- Always verify contract addresses before interacting
- Start with small amounts to test the protocol
- Understand the risks before investing large amounts
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
**Gas Optimization**
- Use gasless transactions with DUSD when available
- Batch multiple operations to save on gas fees
- Consider using Layer 2 networks (Polygon, Arbitrum) for lower costs
{% endhint %}
