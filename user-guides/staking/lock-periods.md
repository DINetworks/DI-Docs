# Lock Periods Guide

Understand how lock periods work in DI Network staking, their benefits, risks, and strategies for optimizing your commitment duration.

## Lock Period Overview

Lock periods are commitment durations where your staked tokens cannot be withdrawn without penalties. They provide:
- **Higher Rewards**: Longer locks earn higher APY multipliers
- **Enhanced Governance**: Increased voting power for longer commitments
- **Protocol Stability**: Reduces token velocity and provides predictable liquidity

## Available Lock Periods

### DI Token Staking Lock Options
| Duration | APY Multiplier | Voting Power | Governance Boost | Early Exit Penalty |
|----------|----------------|--------------|------------------|-------------------|
| **No Lock** | 1.0x | 1.0x | None | None |
| **3 Months** | 1.2x | 1.1x | +10% | 50% of rewards |
| **6 Months** | 1.5x | 1.2x | +20% | 50% of rewards |
| **12 Months** | 2.0x | 1.5x | +50% | 50% of rewards |
| **24 Months** | 2.5x | 2.0x | +100% | 50% of rewards |

### DUSD Staking (Stability Pool)
- **No Lock Period**: DUSD staking has no mandatory lock
- **Flexible Withdrawal**: Can withdraw anytime (subject to pool liquidity)
- **Voluntary Locks**: Optional locks for enhanced rewards (coming soon)

## Choosing Your Lock Period

### Risk Tolerance Assessment
**Conservative (No Lock - 3 Months)**:
- Profile: Risk-averse, need flexibility
- Market View: Uncertain or bearish
- Liquidity Needs: May need funds soon
- Governance: Minimal participation

**Moderate (6-12 Months)**:
- Profile: Balanced risk/reward
- Market View: Cautiously optimistic
- Liquidity Needs: Can commit medium-term
- Governance: Moderate participation

**Aggressive (12-24 Months)**:
- Profile: High conviction, maximum rewards
- Market View: Very bullish long-term
- Liquidity Needs: Don't need funds for years
- Governance: Active participation

### Market Condition Considerations
**Bull Market Strategy**:
```
Market Outlook: Strong upward trend
Recommended: Shorter locks (3-6 months)
Reasoning: Maintain flexibility for opportunities
Risk: Missing out on higher yields
```

**Bear Market Strategy**:
```
Market Outlook: Downward or sideways
Recommended: Longer locks (12-24 months)
Reasoning: Higher yields during difficult times
Risk: Locked during potential recovery
```

**Uncertain Market Strategy**:
```
Market Outlook: Mixed signals
Recommended: Ladder approach
Strategy: Multiple positions with different lock periods
Benefit: Balanced exposure and flexibility
```

## Lock Period Strategies

### Laddering Strategy
**Concept**: Spread stakes across multiple lock periods
```
Laddering Example:
Total Stake: 20,000 DI

Position 1: 5,000 DI - No lock (flexibility)
Position 2: 5,000 DI - 6 months (moderate commitment)
Position 3: 5,000 DI - 12 months (higher rewards)
Position 4: 5,000 DI - 24 months (maximum rewards)

Benefits:
- Diversified risk exposure
- Regular unlock opportunities
- Balanced reward optimization
- Flexibility for market changes
```

### Rolling Strategy
**Concept**: Continuously roll locks as they expire
```
Rolling Example:
Month 0: Lock 10,000 DI for 12 months
Month 12: Unlock and immediately re-lock for 12 months
Month 24: Repeat process

Benefits:
- Consistent high rewards
- Regular reassessment opportunities
- Maintains long-term commitment benefits
- Adapts to changing conditions
```

### Barbell Strategy
**Concept**: Combine maximum flexibility with maximum commitment
```
Barbell Example:
Total Stake: 20,000 DI

Flexible Portion: 10,000 DI - No lock
Committed Portion: 10,000 DI - 24 months

Benefits:
- High rewards from long lock
- Flexibility from unlocked portion
- Ability to capitalize on opportunities
- Risk mitigation
```

## Lock Period Management

### Extending Lock Periods
**When to Extend**:
- Market outlook improves
- Need higher governance power
- Want to maximize rewards
- Confident in protocol future

**Extension Process**:
1. Navigate to staking dashboard
2. Select "Extend Lock Period"
3. Choose new lock duration
4. Confirm transaction
5. New multipliers apply immediately

**Extension Benefits**:
```
Current: 6-month lock (1.5x multiplier)
Extend to: 24-month lock (2.5x multiplier)
Immediate Benefit: 67% increase in reward rate
New Lock Period: Starts from extension date
```

### Early Exit Considerations
**Penalty Structure**:
- **Penalty Amount**: 50% of accrued rewards forfeited
- **Principal Protection**: Original stake always returned
- **Penalty Distribution**: Goes to remaining stakers

**Early Exit Calculation**:
```
Example Early Exit (6 months into 12-month lock):
Original Stake: 10,000 DI
Accrued Rewards: 600 DI
Penalty: 300 DI (50% of rewards)
You Receive: 10,300 DI
Forfeited: 300 DI
```

**When Early Exit Makes Sense**:
- Emergency liquidity needs
- Better opportunities elsewhere (rare)
- Significant protocol concerns
- Major life changes

## Risk Management

### Lock Period Risks
**Liquidity Risk**:
- Cannot access funds during lock
- Opportunity cost of other investments
- Emergency situations

**Market Risk**:
- Token price volatility during lock
- Protocol performance changes
- Competitive landscape shifts

**Protocol Risk**:
- Smart contract vulnerabilities
- Governance decisions affecting rewards
- Regulatory changes

### Risk Mitigation Strategies
**Diversification**:
- Don't lock all tokens at once
- Maintain emergency fund outside protocol
- Diversify across different DeFi protocols

**Gradual Commitment**:
```
Phased Approach:
Month 1: Lock 25% of intended amount
Month 3: Lock additional 25% if satisfied
Month 6: Lock additional 25%
Month 9: Lock final 25%
```

**Emergency Planning**:
- Keep 20% of portfolio unlocked
- Understand early exit penalties
- Have alternative liquidity sources
